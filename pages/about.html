About BookShare

BookShare was created as a platform to help book lovers connect with each other and share their collections. The idea came from my own experience of wanting to read books that were hard to find or expensive to buy.

The mission of BookShare is to create a community of readers who can share their love for books while also being environmentally friendly by reusing and recycling books.
